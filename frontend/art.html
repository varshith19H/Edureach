Creative Arts

Express yourself through various art forms using simple materials. This creative arts course encourages children to explore their creativity through drawing, painting, crafts, and more. Using simple, accessible materials, students will learn basic art techniques while developing self-expression and fine motor skills.

What you'll learn

  • Develop basic drawing and painting skills
  • Learn to use color, line, and shape in artwork
  • Create art using recycled and natural materials
  • Express ideas and feelings through art
